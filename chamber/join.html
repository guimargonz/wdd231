Non-Profit Membership Benefits

  • Listing in online member directory.
  • Networking event access (member rates where applicable).
  • Community advocacy.
  • Cost: No Fee (verification required).

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • All NP benefits.
  • Access to member-only workshops.
  • Discounted rates for paid events.
  • Cost: $100/year.

Silver Membership Benefits

  • All Bronze benefits.
  • Company spotlight opportunity (1 per year, basic listing).
  • Enhanced directory listing.
  • Business referrals.
  • Cost: $250/year.

Gold Membership Benefits

  • All Silver benefits.
  • Premier company spotlight on homepage (random rotation, more frequent).
  • Free admission to select signature events (1 ticket).
  • Logo on Chamber website.
  • Priority consideration for speaking opportunities.
  • Cost: $500/year.
